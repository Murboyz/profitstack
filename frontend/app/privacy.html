The Nut Report collects and processes the minimum business and account information needed to provide reporting, authentication, billing, customer support, and CRM-connected dashboard functionality.

The Nut Report connects to your CRM to access job, sales, and scheduling data. This data is used solely to calculate and display business performance metrics such as production targets, approved sales, and forward-looking scheduling insights.
