Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026

The Nut Report collects and processes the minimum business and account information needed to provide reporting, authentication, billing, customer support, and CRM-connected dashboard functionality.

Information we collect

  • Account details such as name, email, and organization information

  • Authentication, usage, and billing-related records

How we use information

  • To provide dashboard access and reporting
  • To connect and sync approved CRM data sources

Data sharing

We do not sell customer data. We may share data only with service providers required to operate The Nut Report, such as hosting, authentication, database, payment, and infrastructure vendors.

We retain account and reporting data for as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect account and connected business data. No internet-based system can be guaranteed completely secure.
